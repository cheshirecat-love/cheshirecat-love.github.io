CSC/CPE 471 Project - Maddie Morelos

project overview

For my final project, I created a scene of an oil rig at the bottom of the ocean, teeming with wildlife using the OpenGL render pipeline. To compile and run it, there are no additional instructions other than building the directory and running it in the normal way (I have been doing it through the VSCode client, but you can also just do make). The oil rig, shell mounds, fish, and crab models were all modeled/textured by hand by me/reused from my VR game team, and the oil rig is a multi-shape model. For the hierarchical model, I made a person swimming, oscillating up and down and orbiting around the scene. The fish similarly orbit around the environment through the same sin/cos algorithms. I also implemented SDL2 for background music, which is an underwater theme from Pokemon Ruby/Sapphire.
